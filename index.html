Style Influence

In 2018, Earl Sweatshirt collaborated with Knxwledge and released a series of music mixes called "RBMA Radio". I only stumbled upon it in late 2022 and although RBMA Radio no longer exists I listen to it regularly while doing schoolwork or anything else in my room. I love the beats and track selections they suit each other so well and its almost like the songs create a scene and the transitions are so smooth.

My style is heavily influenced by the graphics used in many of the RBMA radio covers. They remind me of the Wojak meme, they are very easy to make and the mind is the only limitation when it comes creating graphics.

My track selections for PHXPS are also very different from Earl and Knxwledge, but all in all I still try to emulate the overall aesthetic that RBMA Radio has.

The Creation Process

PHXPS radio is always based on the impression of the cover art graphic being used for each episode. The music genres then follow to create the aesthetic.

PHXPS Radio cover arts are one of the essential components to PHXPS Radio and often times direct the music composition that will be used in the episode. Similar to looking at art in a museum while being complimented by music.

Music selection varies from episodes but Rap is the most consistent genre that appears on PHXPS radio,

Different music genres will be introduced to the radio the main episodes often times have Rap while the in between episodes have experimental music genres like in the 1.5 episode.

Once the cover art is created and the music tracks have been selected the episode is then recorded in a discord voice chat amongst my friends and I

I beleve this is how the overall laid back vibe is achieved because the commentary doesn't feel artificial and its music we enjoy.

Once the audio clips have been edited the radio is ready to be uploaded to youtube and visible to be seen by viewers!
