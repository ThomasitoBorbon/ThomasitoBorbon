The kickstarters of creativity

The art in my room has many meanings, they all have different stories behind them and are reminders of who I am.

"Bold and Brash"
est.2018

Painted on canvas by my cousin she made this at my request. I had completely forgotten about this idea, than on my birthday I was surprised to see the painting was her gift to me.

"Toro"
est.2020

This was another gift from my cousin it is my personal favorite of the four on display here. Incredible display of skill and the painting is converted from a real life photograph of my father.

"Batman"
est.2013

I can't remember where I got this picture from, all I remember is that its the oldest picture out of all these. I love batman but more specifically his villains. They are the best of any superhero.

"Chappelle"
est.2022

The most recent of the 4. I bought this on Roosevelt street during First Friday, the "Rick James" Chappelle is my favorite skit by far. I like Chappelle or comedians in general who say outlandish things.

Name Description Picture Favorites
Framed Kobe Dunk Kobe Bryant doing a tomahawk dunk against the New Jersey Nets. Kobe inspires me and my peers tremendously I can't remember a time when people were as good at things as they are now because of the "Mamba Mentality". Kobe was a beast. Top 3 Nba player
Cantinflas on a truck This old school red truck sports a stone with a drawing of Cantinflas. Made by dad stones were his canvas varying from drawings, carvings, or forming something out of stone. Quite a formidable artist he is as creative as it gets. Old soul at heart
Sonora State Outline Carved into and drawn by my dad this was one of his bigger pieces that he made and one of my favorites. Proud of where he was from he added the text "Puro Sonora Cabrones" quite the character if you ask me. I to am proud of this heritage as well. These 3 are my favorites of all!
Self-Sketch.jpg While waiting for my lunch at Taco Bell this random guy approaches me and tells me "Hey whats up bro I drew you". The drawing is pretty spot on I feel, the original drawing did not have any color so I put it in photoshop and gave it some pretty nice colors that really make it pop in my opinion. What a strange experience.
Venado and mezquite Deer and Mezquite drawn onto the stone with the outlining carved into it. My dad made all types of things relating to nature and animals, he was a cowboy at heart.
The decorations in my room are all tremendous motivators and inspire me very often, when I feel blue the decor in my room always alleviates me.
