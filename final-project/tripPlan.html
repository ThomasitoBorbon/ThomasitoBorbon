Andaz Tokyo

Andaz Tokyo Toranomon Hills is a luxury hotel located in the prestigious Toranomon Hills skyscraper in Minato ward, Tokyo. It is a popular choice among business and leisure travelers alike due to its central location, excellent facilities, and breathtaking views of the city. The hotel boasts a total of 164 guest rooms and suites, each of which is stylishly decorated and equipped with state-of-the-art amenities.

Guests of the hotel can also enjoy a range of leisure facilities, including a fitness center, a spa, and a swimming pool. The spa offers a range of treatments using natural Japanese ingredients, while the swimming pool is located on the 37th floor and offers stunning views of the city.

The guest rooms and suites at the Andaz Tokyo Toranomon Hills are designed to offer comfort, style, and functionality. Each room features large windows that provide stunning views of Tokyo's skyline, luxurious bedding, and high-quality amenities. Guests can choose from a variety of room types, including standard rooms, suites, and premium rooms that come with exclusive benefits such as access to the Andaz Lounge.

Overall, the Andaz Tokyo Toranomon Hills is a great choice for travelers who want to experience the best of Tokyo in style and comfort.

Capsule Hotel Anshin Oyado Premier Tokyo Shinjuku Station

The Capsule Hotel Anshin Oyado Premier Tokyo Shinjuku Station is a unique concept hotel located in the heart of Tokyo. As its name suggests, the hotel is built around the idea of providing guests with a comfortable capsule-style sleeping experience. Each capsule is designed to provide a private and cozy sleeping space for guests, complete with a personal TV, Wi-Fi, and other amenities.

In addition to the standard capsules, the hotel also offers more spacious "premium" capsules, which include extra amenities such as a massage chair and a private shower. For guests who prefer a bit more space, the hotel also has several traditional rooms available, complete with Japanese-style futon beds.

Park Hyatt Tokyo

Park Hyatt Tokyo is a luxury hotel located in the heart of Shinjuku, one of Tokyo's most vibrant and bustling districts.

The hotel is known for its stunning views of the city, which can be enjoyed from many of its rooms and facilities, including the famous New York Bar on the 52nd floor. The hotel has 177 guest rooms, each elegantly designed with a contemporary style and modern amenities.

Park Hyatt Tokyo has received numerous awards and accolades over the years, including being named one of the "Top 100 Hotels in the World" by Travel + Leisure magazine. The hotel has also been featured in several movies, including "Lost in Translation," which was filmed on location at the hotel.

The Millenials Shibuya

The Millennials Shibuya is a trendy, millennial-focused hotel located in the Shibuya neighborhood of Tokyo. The hotel offers a unique stay experience that combines technology and comfort, making it perfect for young travelers. The hotel features small, private rooms with comfortable beds and modern amenities, as well as a shared lounge area where guests can relax and socialize with each other.

In addition to its high-tech features, The Millennials Shibuya also offers a range of amenities designed to make guests feel at home. There is a fully-equipped kitchen where guests can prepare their own meals, as well as a laundry room and a shower room with toiletries and towels provided. The hotel also offers a complimentary breakfast each morning, with a selection of breads, pastries, and drinks.

Hotel Niwa Tokyo

The Hotel Niwa is a stylish hotel situated in the heart of Tokyo. It boasts a traditional Japanese atmosphere, with modern amenities that cater to both business and leisure travelers. The hotel is located near the Imperial Palace and other major Tokyo landmarks, making it a great base for exploring the city. The hotel's elegant rooms are equipped with comfortable beds, free Wi-Fi, and a flat-screen TV, among other amenities.

One of the highlights of the Hotel Niwa is its beautiful Japanese garden, which provides a peaceful escape from the hustle and bustle of the city. Guests can enjoy a stroll through the garden or relax in one of the comfortable seating areas. The hotel also has a fitness center and a library, providing guests with plenty of entertainment options during their stay.

In addition to its exceptional facilities, the Hotel Niwa is also known for its top-notch dining options. The hotel's signature restaurant, Yukuri, serves exquisite Japanese cuisine made with the freshest local ingredients. Guests can also enjoy a variety of drinks and snacks at the hotel's lounge bar, which features a chic and modern interior design.

For business travelers, the Hotel Niwa offers a range of meeting and event spaces, including a large ballroom and several smaller conference rooms. All of the meeting rooms are equipped with the latest audiovisual technology and high-speed internet access, making them ideal for corporate events, seminars, and workshops. The hotel's dedicated event planning team is also on hand to assist with every aspect of the event, from menu selection to room setup, ensuring that everything runs smoothly and seamlessly.
